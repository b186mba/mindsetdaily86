About Mindset Publisher

Mindset Publisher is a desktop publishing application designed for short-form video creators. It allows authorized creators to connect their social media accounts and publish video content through official platform APIs.

Creator Publishing Tools

The application helps creators prepare and publish original short-form videos to social media platforms they are authorized to use. Users remain in control of their connected accounts and content.

Account Authorization

Users connect their social media accounts through the official authorization flow provided by each platform. Mindset Publisher only performs actions that have been explicitly authorized by the account owner.
