Mindset Publisher is a desktop application that enables creators to securely connect their social media accounts and publish original short-form video content.

Purpose

Mindset Publisher helps creators publish videos to supported social media platforms using each platform's official APIs and OAuth authentication. The application is designed for creators who want a single desktop publishing workflow for managing and publishing their own original content.

Features

  • Secure account authentication using official OAuth flows.
  • Publish creator-owned short-form videos.
  • Support for draft uploads and direct publishing where supported by the platform.
  • Multi-platform publishing workflow.
  • Multi-account support for creators who manage multiple authorized social media accounts.

Account Authorization

Mindset Publisher only accesses social media accounts that have explicitly authorized the application through the platform's official OAuth authorization process. The application never accesses or publishes content to accounts that have not granted permission.

Supported Platforms

  • TikTok
  • YouTube
  • Instagram
