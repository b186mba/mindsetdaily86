Desktop publishing application for creators

Mindset Publisher is a desktop application that helps content creators connect their social media accounts and publish original short-form video content.

Purpose

Mindset Publisher provides a centralized publishing workflow for creators who want to prepare and publish their own short-form videos to supported social media platforms.

The application uses official platform authentication and API integrations to connect creator accounts and perform authorized publishing actions.

Features

  • Secure account connection using official OAuth authentication.
  • Publish original short-form video content.
  • Upload videos as drafts where supported by a platform.
  • Directly publish videos where supported by a platform.
  • Manage publishing workflows across supported social platforms.
  • Support for multiple authorized creator accounts.

Account Authorization

Mindset Publisher only accesses social media accounts that have explicitly authorized the application through the platform's official OAuth authorization process.

The application does not access accounts without authorization and does not publish content to accounts that have not granted permission.

Supported Platforms

  • TikTok
  • YouTube
  • Instagram
