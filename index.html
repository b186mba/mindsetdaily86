Desktop publishing application for creators

Publish short-form video content with Mindset Publisher.

Mindset Publisher is a desktop publishing application that helps short-form video creators connect their authorized social media accounts and publish video content through official platform APIs.

Creator Publishing

Prepare and publish original short-form videos to connected social media platforms using official platform integrations.

Secure Authorization

Creators connect their own social media accounts through the official authorization flow provided by each platform.

Creator-Controlled Content

Creators remain in control of their accounts and content. The application only performs actions authorized by the connected account.

How it works

A creator connects an eligible social media account through the platform's official OAuth authorization flow. After authorization, Mindset Publisher can publish creator-owned short-form video content using the platform's official publishing API.

The application is designed for creators who want to manage their short-form video publishing workflow from a desktop application.
