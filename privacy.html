Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mindset Publisher handles information when authorized users connect social media accounts and use private publishing tools.

1. Information we process

Depending on the connected platform and the permissions granted by the authorized user, the service may process:

  • Basic account information provided by the platform, such as account identifier or display name.
  • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens needed to connect to the platform API.
  • Video files, captions, descriptions, thumbnails, publishing status, and related metadata.
  • Technical logs needed to diagnose upload, authorization, or API errors.

2. How information is used

Information is used only to authorize platform access, upload or manage content requested by the account owner, monitor publishing status, troubleshoot errors, and maintain the publishing workflow.

3. Sharing

The service does not sell personal information. Information may be sent to connected third-party platforms only as needed to perform the requested upload, publishing, authentication, or status-checking action.

4. Storage and security

OAuth tokens and configuration data should be stored securely by the operator and should not be shared publicly. Access to the publishing environment is limited to authorized operators.

5. Data retention

Publishing logs, local configuration files, and uploaded content references may be retained for operational, troubleshooting, and audit purposes. Data that is no longer needed may be deleted by the operator.

6. User control

A connected platform account can revoke access through the platform's own settings or authorization management page. After access is revoked, the service will no longer be able to use that platform API for the account unless authorization is granted again.

7. Children's privacy

The service is not directed to children and is intended for use by authorized account owners or operators only.

8. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as the publishing hub adds new supported accounts, workflows, or platform integrations. The latest version will be posted on this page.
